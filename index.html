About The Loft

A Studio Built On Craft, Not Volume

Tucked on 6th Street in the heart of Redlands, The Loft Hair Studio was founded on a simple belief: great hair is never rushed. Every appointment is booked with intention, every consultation is unhurried, and every guest leaves with a look that's genuinely theirs — not a trend copy-pasted from a phone screen.

Our stylists trained across editorial, bridal, and color-correction work before bringing that precision home to Redlands. We keep our chair count low on purpose, so the studio never feels like a factory — it feels like your favorite well-kept secret.
